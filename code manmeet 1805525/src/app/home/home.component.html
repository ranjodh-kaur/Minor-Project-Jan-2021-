About Us

The bakery opened it's first branch in Sarabha nagar in 2019 and has since propelled a further 2 branches cross-wise over Ludhiana. We offer a wide range of freshly baked products ranging from cakes to breads at reasonable prices and ensure the safest delievery at your door-step.We are not just a bakery, we are not just bakers. In fact, we are just like you, a bunch of food lovers fascinated with sweet indulgence, who dreamt of creating an appetizing fairy land of divine delicacies that relishes the utmost desires.
